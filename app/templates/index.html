Training Data Analysis

According to the graph on the left, more loans are submitted for semiurban properties than urban or rural. However, semiurban loan applications are denied at a lower rate than the other two categories. This relationship is likely due to the fact that individuals living in the suburbs are typically older and more likely to have established careers and/or spouses with a second income.

Most loan applications are relatively similar to one another. The average loan amount is around $120,000 and most applications do not deviate far from that node. Further, most applications are for 30 year mortgages.

A 30 year window gives lenders the confidence that applicants will be able to repay the principal with interest. It also allows applicants to maximize present purchasing power by leveraging future earnings.
